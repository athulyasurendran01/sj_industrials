PROFILE

         M/s. St. Joseph’s Industrials started in 1992 with the aim of manufacturing Various types of Tile factory Machineries like Pugmills, Revolving Tile Presses, Friction Presses, Disintegrator etc….. Environment Friendly Equipments to satisfy modern industrial needs St.Joseph’s Industrials the pioneers in “Tile Factory Machineries” has extensive customers mainly Tile Factory Industries. For the last 15 years St.Joseph’s industrials has created and improved different types &size of Decorative Tile Factory Machineries. St.Joseph’s industrials undertakes design, development and production of specialized equipments to the tile factory Industries. St.Joseph’s Industrials can undertake complete turnkey project in the field of Decorative Tiles. We also undertake projects of Interlock Bricks mfg. Units and Hollow block mfg. units.
