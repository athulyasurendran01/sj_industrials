    • Tile Making Hydraulic Press

    This machine consists a rigid steel body mounted with suitable M.S. angle stand. It has a central screw made from quality mild steel. A pressure valve is provided for safety purpose. This machine is tested up to the pressure of 4000 lbs per square inch and desirable workable pressure is between 2110 to 2700 lbs/Sq.inch. A cast iron fly wheel has been provided for easy operation. To improve the quality of the machine, entire hydraulic attachment and fittings are made from EN 8/OHS material components. This machine is working by oil with a production capacity of 2500 tiles for 8 hour by four manpower.

    Double vibrated or Hand operated Concrete Block Making Machine
